# boden_check_nrw
Findet die Bodenbeschaffenheit zu einer Adresse heraus. 

The goal is to give insight on the type of soil. 
Even though it can't describe the soil without uncertainty because of lack of information about added soil and compression because of heavy machinery used for construction, 
we, however, increase the chance of proper preparation and use of tools for the job and be more specific about the offer and more precise about our cost calculation. 
On top of that, the data used is meant to be expanded on any work on soil in the future, like sealing walls . 

The big picture includes analytical work as a service and adding a advisory depth to the company in order to reduce physical labor.
